ABOUT US

Building software that respects your time

Kaklotar Studio started as a one-person effort to make Android apps that do exactly what they promise — nothing more, nothing less.

Our Mission

To build lightweight, honest Android apps that solve one real problem well, without wasting a user's storage, data, or attention.

Our Vision

A small, trusted catalog of apps that people recommend to others because they simply work — not because they were forced to leave a rating.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Our values

Clarity over cleverness

An app should be understandable in the first ten seconds of use.

Respect for data

We ask for the minimum permissions needed, every time, no exceptions.

Long-term maintenance

Publishing an app is the start of a commitment, not the end of one.

Honest monetization

Ads keep apps free — never at the cost of misleading the user.
