Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kaklotar Studio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use any app we publish on Google Play (each, an "App"), or when you visit this website. It applies to every App listed on our Apps page unless a specific App's Play Store listing states a different policy.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on the App, we may collect:

  • Usage data: non-personal information such as app interactions, screen views, and session length, used to understand how the App is used.
  • Device information: device model, operating system version, and language settings, used for compatibility and bug fixing.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs: technical data automatically collected when an App crashes or encounters an error, used only to fix the issue.
  • Permissions-based data: if an App requests a permission (for example, camera access for a QR scanner), that data is used only to power the specific feature it was requested for, and is not stored on our servers beyond what is required for the feature to function.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, or precise health data.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the core functionality of each App.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance and stability.
  • To understand aggregate usage trends so we can prioritize improvements.
  • To respond to support requests you send us directly.

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Free Apps may display ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time from your device's Settings app.

Apps may also use Firebase (a Google service) for crash reporting and basic analytics. Data shared with these services is subject to Google's own privacy policy in addition to this one.

4. Data Storage & Security

Where data is stored outside your device, we use reputable third-party infrastructure (such as Google Firebase) with industry-standard security practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain usage and diagnostic data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. To request deletion of any data associated with your use of an App, email umangkaklotar01@gmail.com with the App name — requests are processed within 7 business days.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking, reset your advertising identifier, or revoke individual app permissions at any time through your device's Settings. Uninstalling an App removes all locally stored data associated with it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our Apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of an App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or a specific App's data practices can be sent to umangkaklotar01@gmail.com.
